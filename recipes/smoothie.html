All-Fruit Smoothies

The best fruit smoothie

Quick, easy smoothies made entirely with fruit!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Pour pineapple juice into a blender
  2. Add banana, strawberries, and blueberries
  3. Cover and blend until smooth
  4. Pour into 2 glasses
